History of Baby V.O.X

  • 1997 – Original Lineup: Kim E-Z (Leader/Rapper), Lee Hee-jin (Lead Vocalist), Cha Yumi (Main Vocalist), Jung Shi-woon (Rapper), Jeong Hyeon-jeong (Sub-Vocalist). Debut album: Equalizeher, single "Hair Cut".
  • 1998 – Baby V.O.X II: Cha Yumi leaves due to injury; replaced by Kan Mi-youn. Jung Shi-woon and Jeong Hyeon-jeong replaced by Shim Eun-jin and Lee Gai. Album Baby V.O.X II released with hit "Ya Ya Ya".
  • 1999 – Come Come Come Baby: Lee Gai leaves; Yoon Eun-hye joins. Album Come Come Come Baby released, featuring hit "Missing You".
  • 2001: Album Boyish Story released, including singles "Game Over" and "Doll".
  • 2003: Album Devotion released, featuring hit "What Should I Do".
  • 2004 – Ride West: Final album Ride West released, including the song "Xcstasy". Shim Eun-jin leaves the group.
  • 2005: Yoon Eun-hye and Kim E-Z leave the group.
  • February 5, 2006: Baby V.O.X officially disbands.
  • December 12, 2024: Reunion performance on KBS’s End of the Year Broadcast.
  • September 26–27, 2025: Solo concert scheduled at Peace Hall, Kyung Hee University, Seoul.
